About

KICTM (Karnival ICT dan Matematik) is an annual ICT and Mathematics Carnival held by Universiti Teknologi Mara, Cawangan Melaka, Kampus Jasin.
This year, this carnival is held from 27th to 29th September and the motto is #nurturingUnboundedICT.
